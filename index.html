Who This Is For

This isn't for everyone. That's the point.

The honest filter. If you see yourself in the left column, we should talk. If you see yourself in the right — no hard feelings, it just isn't the right fit.

You might be a fit if —

  • You're tired of trading hours for a ceiling
  • You're coachable and ready to learn
  • You're willing to put in 90 days of real work
  • You want to build something that's yours
  • You're the kind of person people trust
  • You're in Ontario or willing to network here

This isn't for you if —

  • You're looking for a quick flip
  • You want someone to hand you clients
  • You're not open to feedback
  • You want a 9-to-5 with a guaranteed paycheck
  • You think "networking" is a dirty word
  • You're not ready to get licensed
